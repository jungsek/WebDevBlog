Best Steak and Eggs Recipe

Steak and eggs, a classic culinary combination found on breakfast and brunch menus for years, delivers a fusion of flavors and textures to kickstart your day. In a nutshell, steak and eggs is a hearty and indulgent breakfast choice when executed with precision. It's all about the right cut, bold seasoning, and that perfect runny yolk. Don't mess it up, or I'll be yelling at you as if it's MasterChef. Now, get cooking!

Ingredients

  1. A5 Wagyu Australian Imported Beef Steak
  2. Two tbsp. Olive oil
  3. One tsp. Kosher salt
  4. Freshly ground Black pepper
  5. Two tbsp. Butter
  6. Four eggs

To nail this dish, begin with the right steak cut – a well-marbled, juicy ribeye or New York strip. Season generously with bold salt and pepper. Heat a smoking hot pan, sear the steak to your preferred doneness (medium-rare for me), and let it rest. For the eggs, go sunny-side-up with a gloriously runny yolk. Fry them in the same pan with the steak juices for extra flavor. Serve the ensemble on a warm plate, garnished with fresh herbs like chives or parsley for a burst of color and freshness.

Enjoy!
